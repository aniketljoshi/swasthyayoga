# Swasthya Yoga Center

Welcome to the Swasthya Yoga Center website! This site is designed to provide information about our yoga center, programs, teachers, and how you can get in touch or visit us in Chinchwad, Pimpri-Chinchwad, Maharashtra.

## About the Website
- **Modern, Responsive Design:** Built with React, Tailwind CSS, and Vite for a fast, mobile-friendly experience.
- **Sections:**
  - **Hero:** Welcoming introduction to Swasthya Yoga Center.
  - **About:** Learn about our philosophy and approach to yoga.
  - **Programs:** Overview of yoga classes and offerings.
  - **Schedule:** Class timings and weekly schedule.
  - **Teacher:** Meet our experienced yoga instructors.
  - **Gallery:** Photos and highlights from our center and events.
  - **Testimonials:** Feedback from our community and students.
  - **Contact:** Location, contact details, and opening hours. Includes a Google Maps embed for easy navigation.
  - **Footer:** Quick links and additional information.
